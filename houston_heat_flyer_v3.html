How to Stay Safe
💧
Stay Hydrated Drink water every 15–20 min outdoors. Don’t wait until you’re thirsty.
🏢
Use Cooling Centers Find free AC at libraries, community centers & City of Houston cooling sites.
🤝
Check on Neighbors Check on elderly neighbors and relatives daily during heat advisories.
⏰
Avoid Peak Hours Limit outdoor activity 10 AM–4 PM. Plan errands for early morning.
👕
Dress for the Heat Wear light, loose-fitting, light-colored clothing and a wide-brimmed hat.
🚨
Know the Signs Heavy sweating, weakness, fast pulse — call 911 for confusion or loss of consciousness.
